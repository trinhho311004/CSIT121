Slice of Life

The slice of life genre in comic books is a type of storytelling that focuses on the everyday experiences of ordinary people. It often portrays realistic and relatable situations, such as work, school, and relationships, and may explore themes of personal growth, self-discovery, and the complexities of human emotion. The genre may feature minimal plot and character development, instead emphasizing the mundane aspects of daily life.
