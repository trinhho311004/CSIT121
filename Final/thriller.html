Thriller

The thriller genre in comic books is a type of storytelling that focuses on suspenseful and often dangerous situations. It often features protagonists who are in danger or facing difficult challenges, such as solving a crime, stopping a terrorist plot, or surviving a disaster. The genre may incorporate elements of other genres, such as action, horror, or mystery, and is characterized by its fast-paced plot, unexpected plot twists, and intense emotional impact.
