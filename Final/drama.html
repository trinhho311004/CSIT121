Drama

The drama genre is characterized by serious and emotional storytelling that often explores complex themes and interpersonal relationships. It can be found in many different mediums, including plays, novels, films, and comic books. Through conflicts and inner struggles, drama aims to engage the audience on an emotional level and encourage reflection on the human condition.
