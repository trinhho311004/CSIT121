Romance

The romance genre in comic books is a type of storytelling that focuses on the emotional and romantic relationships between characters. It often portrays the ups and downs of love, exploring themes of attraction, infatuation, and heartbreak. The genre may also incorporate elements of other genres, such as drama or comedy, and can be found in many different styles and formats.
