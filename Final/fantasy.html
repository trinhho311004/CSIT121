Fantasy

The fantasy genre in comic books is a type of storytelling that features imaginative and often supernatural elements. It may include magical creatures, mythical realms, or characters with extraordinary abilities or powers. The genre often allows for exploration of themes and ideas that are impossible in the real world, and may involve epic battles between good and evil, quests for magical artifacts, or journeys into uncharted territories.
