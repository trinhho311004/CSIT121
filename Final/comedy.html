Comedy

The comedy genre in comic books is a type of storytelling that aims to make the reader laugh and feel amused. It often involves witty dialogue, humorous situations, and exaggerated or absurd characters. While some comedies may have serious undertones or explore deeper themes, the primary focus is on providing entertainment through humor.
