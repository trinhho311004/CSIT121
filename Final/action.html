Action

The action genre in comic books is a type of storytelling that emphasizes physical combat, daring feats, and thrilling adventure. It often features heroic characters engaged in high-stakes battles against evil villains or dangerous forces. The genre may incorporate elements of other genres, such as science fiction, fantasy, or espionage, and is characterized by fast-paced action sequences and dramatic plot twists.
